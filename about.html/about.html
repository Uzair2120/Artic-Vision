About Us

Our History

Founded in the August of 1948, Pak Carpets Industries began its journey with the trade of handmade carpets, soon becoming Pakistan’s first dedicated exporter of handmade carpet. With an award winning heritage of innovation that spans over seven decades, since then we have become more than just one of Pakistan’s leading flooring providers; We offer our valued and varied clients tailor made holistic interior solutions as well a wide range of furniture options, along with unmatched service to empower their success for a lifetime.

Our Mantra

At PCI, we aspire to a simple, all-encompassing philosophy: To Design Positive Spaces. Whether it’s through our prestigious partnerships with global brands, our expertise in innovation or our artful sense of aesthetics we live that perspective; With our range world class services and products, from state-of-the-art carpeting and exclusive flooring products to total interior solutions, we help you craft exquisite and engaging spaces to live, work and play in comfort through our experience, our passion for detail and adherence to a truly global standard.
